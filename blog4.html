Goa has plenty of affordable hostels and guesthouses, especially in backpacker-friendly areas like Anjuna, Vagator, Baga, and Palolem. These hostels offer dormitory-style stays, private rooms, and common areas where travelers can connect and share experiences. Many budget accommodations come with free Wi-Fi, basic breakfast, and helpful staff who can guide you to lesser-known spots. If you book in advance, you can get amazing deals during peak season. Staying in such hostels not only saves money but also allows you to meet like-minded travelers.
