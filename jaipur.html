Discover Jaipur

About Jaipur

Jaipur, the Pink City of India, is known for its royal palaces, grand forts, and colorful markets. It offers a glimpse into India's rich history, vibrant culture, and architectural brilliance.

Top Attractions

  • Amber Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace
  • Jal Mahal

Best Time to Visit

October to March is the best time to explore Jaipur due to its pleasant weather.
