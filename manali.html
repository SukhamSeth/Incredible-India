Discover Manali

About Manali

Manali is a picturesque hill station nestled in the Himalayas, known for its snow-capped mountains, adventure sports, and vibrant culture. It is a paradise for trekking, skiing, and nature lovers.

Top Attractions

  • Solang Valley
  • Rohtang Pass
  • Hadimba Temple
  • Old Manali

Best Time to Visit

October to June is ideal for pleasant weather and adventure activities.
